This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard for analyzing Sales performance, Revenue, and Total Margin. The dashboard provides key insights and opportunities for optimization using various KPIs and visualizations.

Purpose:
To Unlock Sales insights that are not visiblebefor for sales team for decision suppoer tand automate them to reduced manual time spent in data gathering 

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) The dashboard focuses on the following primary KPIs:
Total Sales: Overall revenue generated from all items sold ($98.8M) Average Sales: Average revenue per sale ($2) Total Profit Margin(2465.7M)
Used Bar charts and column charts to show the sales and  Revenue by products,market and date.
Used Slicers to show the revenue analysis over period of time.
Revenue and Profit margins are shown in bar and area carts along with the custome details in table.
Features Filter Panel: Allows users to filter data by Date/year, OrderID,Product and Region.sss

End Result:
An Automated Dashboard providing quick and latest sales insights in order to support data driven decision making.
Sales Team able to take better decisions prove 10% cost savings of total spend.
Sales analysts stop data gathering manually in order to save 20% of their business time and reinvest it value added activity.
